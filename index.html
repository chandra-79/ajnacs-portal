About Us

At Ajna Consulting Services (AjnaCS), our experts bring together over 50 years of combined experience delivering enterprise-level solutions, guiding technology roadmaps, and optimizing IT infrastructures. We’ve collaborated extensively with leadership teams to define strategies, evaluate new products, and ensure each initiative aligns with organizational objectives.

Our consultants have served as primary stakeholders in P1/Sev1 WAR rooms, providing real-time, business-critical solutions and orchestrating cross-functional teams to meet urgent demands. Beyond crisis management, we’ve designed and developed hybrid (on-premises to cloud) reference architectures and led the migration of legacy applications to Azure, AWS, and Alibaba Cloud—conducting POCs and performance benchmarking to ensure seamless deployments.

We pride ourselves on creating comprehensive monitoring solutions—from disk and CPU usage to I/O, networks, application servers, databases, and load balancers—complete with automated alerts to maximize system uptime. Our team has supervised stress testing and performance tuning, boosting reliability and throughput across enterprise environments.

Hands-on implementation is at the heart of what we do, as we tackle complex client projects with strategic foresight—aligning each technical decision with broader business goals. We also support sales, marketing, professional services, and training, ensuring technology excellence underpins every facet of our clients’ operations.

Whether educating customers on Oracle FMW, open-source technologies, databases, enterprise engineering systems, or Big Data, we continuously create detailed technical documentation to share knowledge across industries. We commit to providing best-practice recommendations, performing architecture reviews, and ensuring full compliance with industry standards—so you can trust that AjnaCS delivers exceptional, future-ready IT solutions every time.
